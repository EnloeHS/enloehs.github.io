Our Message

Enloe High School prepares all students for the global society by giving them a broad spectrum of opportunities to prepare for education and employment beyond high school. Enloe High School is a community dedicated to a standard of excellence in education that fosters scholarship, integrity, diversity and equity.

Enloe High School in partnership with Communities in Schools and C.O.N.C.E.R.T, is pleased to offer, Eagle Academy

Eagle Academy is a after-school enrichment program geared towards delivering individualized academic support and instruction in Math and Language arts. In addition to core instruction, students will be exposed to daily Project Based STEM activities to engage and challenge their thinking. Eagle Academy meets daily Monday thru Thursday 2:45-5:30. If you are interested in enrolling your child; Click here to pre-register. If you have any additional questions contact Charon Williams at cwilliams@ciswake.org .

Homebase Student Access 2015-2016

Beginning July 6, 2015, student access to Home Base/PowerSchool will be replaced by a single sign-on process offered through NCEdCloud. Students must claim their account through the NCEdCloud to access PowerSchool.

To claim your NCEdCloud Account, visit http://my.ncedcloud.org using Google Chrome or Firefox and follow the instructions to "Claim My Account".

There are no log in changes for Homebase Parent Access.

New applications and those received after August 13 will be processed and/or available for pick up on Friday, August 21 from 9-11 and during lunches on Monday, August 24, Tuesday, August 25, and Wednesday, August 26.
